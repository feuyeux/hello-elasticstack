### 2. Download ES stack
```shell
STACK_VERSION=5.2.0
ES_TAR=elasticsearch-${STACK_VERSION}.tar.gz
LS_TAR=logstash-${STACK_VERSION}.tar.gz
KB_TAR=kibana-${STACK_VERSION}-linux-x86_64.tar.gz
FB_TAR=filebeat-${STACK_VERSION}-linux-x86_64.tar.gz

axel -n 5 -a -S5 "https://artifacts.elastic.co/downloads/elasticsearch/${ES_TAR}"
axel -n 5 -a -S5 "https://artifacts.elastic.co/downloads/kibana/${KB_TAR}"
axel -n 5 -a -S5 "https://artifacts.elastic.co/downloads/logstash/${LS_TAR}"
axel -n 5 -a -S5 "https://artifacts.elastic.co/downloads/beats/filebeat/${FB_TAR}"

mkdir okie_dokie
mv *.tar.gz okie_dokie
```
